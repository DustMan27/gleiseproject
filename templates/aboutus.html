About Us

Founded in 2050, TerraSpace is at the forefront of humanity's next great adventure: the colonization of distant worlds. For over 50 years, we have dedicated ourselves to the mission of finding a new home for humanity beyond Earth. As the challenges facing our planet grow, our goal is clear—to ensure the survival and prosperity of the human race by expanding our horizons to the stars.

With a team of visionary scientists, engineers, and explorers, TerraSpace has spent decades developing cutting-edge technology and sustainable strategies to make interstellar travel a reality. Our flagship project is the journey to Gliese 12 b, an exoplanet with the potential to support human life. This ambitious endeavor is more than just a space mission; it’s a beacon of hope for future generations.

At TerraSpace, we believe in the power of exploration, innovation, and resilience. We are driven by a single purpose: to save humanity by providing a new frontier for human civilization. Join us as we embark on this historic journey and make the stars our new home. Together, we can build a brighter future—one that reaches beyond the limits of Earth.
