About Us

Gliese 12 b is a super Earth exoplanet that orbits a M-type star. Its mass is 3.87 Earths, it takes 12.8 days to complete one orbit of its star, and is 0.0668 AU from its star. Its discovery was announced in 2024.

Along with the planets of TRAPPIST-1, Gliese 12 b is one of the nearest known relatively temperate transiting exoplanets. Gliese 12 b orbits slightly closer than the inner edge of its star's habitable zone, with an insolation between those of Earth and Venus. Its equilibrium temperature, assuming zero albedo, is 315 K (42 °C; 107 °F).
